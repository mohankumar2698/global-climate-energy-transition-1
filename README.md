# Global Climate & Energy Transition Analysis (2000–2026)

## Overview

This project explores global climate trends and the energy transition from fossil fuels to renewable energy between 2000 and 2026.

The dataset includes:

- Global CO₂ emissions
- CO₂ emissions per capita
- Fossil fuel energy consumption
- Renewable energy consumption
- Global temperature anomalies
- Energy intensity
- Carbon intensity
- Renewable energy share

## Objectives

- Analyze climate trends
- Measure renewable energy growth
- Study decarbonization progress
- Visualize long-term sustainability indicators

## Dataset

Location:

data/Global_Climate_Energy_Transition_Project_2000_2026.csv

## Key Questions

1. How have CO₂ emissions changed over time?
2. How rapidly is renewable energy growing?
3. Is carbon intensity decreasing?
4. What is the relationship between temperature anomalies and emissions?
